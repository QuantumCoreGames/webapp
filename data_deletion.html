Data Deletion Policy

This page is provided to comply with the data deletion request requirements set forth by Facebook.

Our App's Data Collection Policy

We want to be perfectly clear about how your data is handled:

  • No Explicit Data Collection: Our application is designed to function without collecting, storing, or processing any personal user data. It does not require a login, user accounts, or any explicit personal information (such as name, email, or physical location) to operate.
  • No Data Storage: Because we do not collect data, there is no user data stored on our servers for deletion.

Third-Party Data Collection

While our application itself does not collect personal data, it does integrate with third-party services that may collect and process data for operational purposes:

  • Advertising (AdMob): We use Google AdMob for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect data (such as device identifiers and IP address) to serve relevant ads and measure performance. Please refer to the Google Privacy Policy for more details.
  • Social Platform Integration (Facebook): Integration with Facebook is limited to necessary platform functions. Facebook may collect data according to their own policies. Please refer to the Facebook Data Policy for details on their data handling and deletion processes.

Data Deletion Request

If you have interacted with our app and wish to formally request the deletion of any data that might have been collected by the third-party platforms (Facebook, AdMob, etc.) through our app, please contact us directly.

To initiate a deletion request, please email us at:
